1. Copywriters:

Skillsgrow is seeking to employ a small number of professional copywriters. We have a great story to tell, and Word of Mouth has been how Skillsgrow has expanded with so little investment over the past few years. However, how a story is told makes all the difference. We need writers to help us with the clear and crisp narrative of functional pages, to the inspirational words that help convince people how they can indeed improve their lives through self-motivated study. If you would like to be part of the Skillsgrow copywriting team, drop us a line, sharing with us the requirements below, but also share with us samples of your work and tell us about the work you have done that is broadly similar to what you think might be appropriate at Skillsgrow.

We have learners in every country worldwide, so many, if not most of our learners do not use English, our main language, as their first language day to day. You would need to write for every reader, not just the native English reader. While we want to have copywriters close to our marketing and technical teams, that can possibly be virtual, it may be the case that experienced writers might be able to be located remotely. Our first preference though is that you can at least come to our Chennai office from time to time to meet the team and understand the breath of what we do and how we do it.
