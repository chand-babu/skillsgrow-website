Register your free Skillsgrow account

You can register your Skillsgrow account either using your email address, or through a social media account. Registering an account is completely free and gives you unlimited access to the Skillsgrow website. Once you’ve registered your account, upload a picture, update your profile and make it public. Your Skillsgrow profile lets you boast about your skills, experience, and Skillsgrow Certification to anyone you need to share it with, including prospective employers, friends and family.

Find the right course

Skillsgrow has over 100 job ready courses available, all absolutely free to complete, and new courses are added weekly.

Get Enrolled

Once you have found the course or Learning Path you want to study, all you have to do is click on the Login button to enrol and get learning. All Skillsgrow courses are self-paced, so you can study whenever you want, wherever you want. Manage your time, track your learning and see your knowledge grow with our practical learner dashboard.

Become a Graduate

You can take or re-take an assessment on Skillsgrow anytime you want, but we require scores of 60% or more to pass. Once you have passed all the assessments on your course, you are officially a Skillsgrow graduate!

Acquire your Certification

Once you have completed your course, you can purchase your Certificate or Diploma (depending on the type of course or Learning Path you’ve completed) and we will mail it to you. Your Skillsgrow Certification can be included with your CV/Resume to share with potential employers, or just to share your achievement with the world!
